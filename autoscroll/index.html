Humanaties Impact On Nature From Above

Every since the late 1940s humans have unlocked a new perspective of Earth. From above, from space, from orbiting satellites (and people too).

Years of imagery have recorded the lands we live on; the whites of clouds; and the vast blue oceans. Beauty. But…they also have captured changes we’ve made to our Earth. A lot, of those changes having no iota of actual wisdom. Careless destruction of the environment, to our own detriment.

Is it not our obligation to stop this?
